Rock Paper Scissors Game


Rock Paper Scissors is a simple game that is often used to determine a winner in a casual setting. This game you are playing against the computer. The winner is determined by the rules below:


  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

If both players choose the same shape, the game is a tie. To play the game, both players choose one of the three hand signals representing Rock, Paper or Scissors. The winner of the round is then determined by the rules above and the players can play again. The scoreboard will show the results of the previous games played. To reset game and start again just press the reset game button below the Hand Gestures in the game area. First Player to 5 wins the game.
